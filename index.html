About

My name is Angelina Fischer, I’m 26 and I am a starting web developer from Darmstadt, Germany.
When I finished my master in Materials Science at the Technical University Darmstadt in April 2020, I was struggling to find a job due to the COVID-19 crisis. After realizing this is not the end of the world, I took this as an opportunity to become a self-tought web developer!

My goal is to become a full-stack engineer and I am currently learning about more advanced JavaScript, jQuery, React, and back-end development.
